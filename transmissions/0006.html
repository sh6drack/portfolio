LOOPS AND OVERLAYS

most people are trapped inside loops they never volunteered for. loops built from associations they never examined.

they say they want change but act like reality is external, like it's something they have to chase or fix. but what they call "life" is just an overlay. an ongoing projection of their internal conditions. their beliefs. their convictions about how hard it is, how distant they are from what they want, how unworthy they must be to not already have it.

it's mechanical. experience filters through belief. and belief shapes what you even allow in.

most people's minds are full. but not with thoughts, just ***unprocessed input.*** timelines, content, stress, backlog. the thoughts never even get finished. they're like emails you never open, piling up until your whole internal system is flagged.

silence isn't peace. it's exposure. and people avoid it like death.
