SATAN IS OF GOD

the biggest blasphemy isn’t denying god. it’s pretending god has an opposite.

people say satan is against god. outside of god. a force that exists in contradiction to divine order. but if god is totality, if god is all, then what could possibly be outside that? what system are you invoking where something can oppose an infinite presence without being inside it?

satan isn’t against god. satan is a function of god. not a rival, but a role. the tester. the adversary. the distortion that reveals the structure.

when people think they’re being attacked by something “evil,” they’re just experiencing the friction of their own resistance. the misunderstanding of their own internal architecture. the chaos isn’t external. it’s a feedback loop between their expectations and what reality is actually showing them.

blame satan and you get to stay the victim. but when you realize satan is in the blueprint, you’re forced to confront what part of you keeps building traps and calling them enemies.

nothing’s out to get you. you’re just interpreting correction as punishment.
